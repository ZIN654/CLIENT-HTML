NAMBALAN RIVER

Nambalan Beach, tucked away in Tarlac province, Philippines, is a serene haven with its powdery white sands and clear waters. Located in Brgy. Nambalan, Camiling, it offers an escape for swimming, sunbathing, and peaceful picnics. Travelers usually reach it by land, taking a bus or driving from Tarlac city center to Camiling, then seeking directions to Brgy. Nambalan for a tranquil seaside retreat.

MONASTERIO DE TARLAC

Monasterio de Tarlac, located in Tarlac, Philippines, is a renowned religious site atop Mount Resurrection in San Jose municipality. Accessible by road from Tarlac City or nearby towns, it features a striking 30-foot statue of the Risen Christ and offers a serene atmosphere for prayer and reflection amidst breathtaking views.

AQUINO CENTER  AND MUSEUM

The AQUINO Center and Museum in Tarlac City, Philippines, pays tribute to Senator Ninoy and President Cory Aquino. Accessible by land, visitors can reach it via private or public transportation. The museum stands as a significant cultural and historical landmark in the city.
